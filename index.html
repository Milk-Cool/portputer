FAQ

QUESTION
how many hours are required for getting one?
ANSWER
depends on the shipping costs; typically 6-8 hours, the exact formula is 6hr + (delivery US$ / 5)hr
QUESTION
what kinds of projects are accepted?
ANSWER
any desktop app or esp32-based app that can be theoretically ported to a cardputer! please keep in mind that this is an esp32s3, which not only has just 4MB of flash, but also less than 1MB of RAM. please take a look at the store page linked above if you wanna learn more about the hardware!
QUESTION
can i get any additional addons?
ANSWER
yes!! the more hours you code for, the more credits you receive for buying not just the cardputer, but also the lora cap and any other compatible modules! (you can find them all on the m5stack store :D)
